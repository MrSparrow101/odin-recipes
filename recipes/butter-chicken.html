Butter Chicken

Description

Indulge in the creamy and luxurious taste of Butter Chicken, one of the most celebrated dishes in Indian cuisine. Tender chicken pieces are marinated in a flavorful blend of yogurt, spices, and lemon juice before being cooked to perfection. The rich tomato-based sauce, enhanced with butter and cream, creates a velvety texture that perfectly complements the spiced chicken. The balance of tangy tomatoes, aromatic spices, and creamy richness makes this dish a true delight for the senses.

Whether paired with naan, roti, or steamed rice, Butter Chicken is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser. Its rich and smooth gravy, paired with succulent chicken pieces, brings warmth and comfort to every meal. Ideal for both festive occasions and cozy dinners, this timeless recipe is a must-try for anyone who appreciates authentic flavors.

Ingredients:

For The Chicken Marinade:

  • 1 kg chicken, boneless
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste

For The Sauce:

  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4-5 cloves
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste

For Garnish:

  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • 1 tbsp butter

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Combine chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, red chili powder, lemon juice, and salt. Let it marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Cook marinated chicken in a pan until fully cooked.
  3. Prepare the Sauce: In a separate pan, melt butter and saut√© onions. Add tomato puree, garam masala, sugar, and salt. Cook until thickened.
  4. Combine Chicken and Sauce: Add cooked chicken to the sauce and stir in heavy cream.
  5. Simmer: Let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. Serve: Garnish with coriander and butter. Serve with naan or rice.
